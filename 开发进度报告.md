**衣橱 (Wardrobe)**
- [WardrobeViewController.swift](apps/ios/WhatToWear/Features/Wardrobe/WardrobeViewController.swift) - 分类筛选、网格展示
- [AddClothingViewController.swift](apps/ios/WhatToWear/Features/Wardrobe/AddClothingViewController.swift) - 图片上传、AI抠图、分类选择

**穿搭 (Outfit)**
- [OutfitViewController.swift](apps/ios/WhatToWear/Features/Outfit/OutfitViewController.swift) - 2D/3D切换、换搭/保存/日历
- [OutfitCalendarViewController.swift](apps/ios/WhatToWear/Features/Outfit/OutfitCalendarViewController.swift) - 月历、穿搭记录

**试穿 (TryOn)**
- [TryOnViewController.swift](apps/ios/WhatToWear/Features/TryOn/TryOnViewController.swift) - 3D上身、手势微调（缩放/肩线/衣长）

**个人中心 (Profile)**
- [ProfileViewController.swift](apps/ios/WhatToWear/Features/Profile/ProfileViewController.swift) - 菜单列表
- [BodyModelViewController.swift](apps/ios/WhatToWear/Features/Profile/BodyModelViewController.swift) - 身型录入（模板/AI/手动）
- [MemberCenterViewController.swift](apps/ios/WhatToWear/Features/Profile/MemberCenterViewController.swift) - VIP会员、特权、课程
- [CoursesViewController.swift](apps/ios/WhatToWear/Features/Profile/CoursesViewController.swift) - 穿搭课程列表
- [SettingsViewController.swift](apps/ios/WhatToWear/Features/Profile/SettingsViewController.swift) - 设置、退出登录

**AI服务**
- [WeatherService.swift](apps/ios/WhatToWear/Core/AI/WeatherService.swift) - 定位、天气、穿搭推荐
- [OutfitGenerationService.swift](apps/ios/WhatToWear/Core/AI/OutfitGenerationService.swift) - 智能搭配生成
